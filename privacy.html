Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-12 · Heisenbug Inc.

Heisenbug Inc. ("we," "Heisenbug") runs the website at heisenbug.app and the demos at heisenbug.app/demos/. This policy describes what we collect when you visit, and what we do with it. Plain language, no dark patterns.

What we collect

  • Your email address, only when you send us one — the contact CTAs on the site open your email client and you type a message to hello@heisenbug.app. We don't intercept that mail; it arrives in our Google Workspace inbox.
  • Basic server logs from GitHub Pages (the platform that serves heisenbug.app) — request paths, timestamps, user-agent strings. These are retained per GitHub's standard practices and are not linked to identifiable visitors by us.
  • No cookies set by Heisenbug. No tracking pixels, no third-party analytics, no remarketing tags.

What we do with it

  • When you email us, we reply. That's it.
  • We may keep your email and conversation as a record so we can pick the conversation back up later. We do not add you to any marketing list without your explicit consent.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share contact data with third parties.

If we build a cockpit for you

When we engage to build a cockpit, our handling of your business data is governed by the project agreement between us, not by this website privacy policy. Standard practices for that work include:

  • Your business data lives in a database hosted in infrastructure we operate on your behalf (typically Supabase, Cloudflare, or Deno Deploy in US regions).
  • For deployments involving regulated data (HIPAA, PCI), we work with you on the appropriate Business Associate Agreement (BAA) or equivalent before any production data is loaded.
  • You own your data. You can request an export or deletion at any time during or after the engagement.

Children

This site and our services are not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children.

Your rights

You have the right to know what we hold about you, to request a copy, and to ask us to delete it. To do any of those, email hello@heisenbug.app with your request. We respond within 30 days.

Updates to this policy

If we change this policy materially we'll update the "Last updated" date and, where reasonable, notify you. The page you are reading now is the current version.
