Recommendation engine — how it picks

The system looks at three signals for each milestone: (1) the client's historical preferences (cut, stone, era, color), (2) what's currently in your safe matching those preferences, and (3) the family's known budget patterns from past purchases. It surfaces the best match plus 2-3 alternates.

1
Preference signal: per-client style profile (auto-learned + manually editable)
EX: GOLDBERG = CUSHION CUTS, D COLOR, MINIMUM 2.5CT
2
Inventory match: live filter over your safe contents
PULLED FROM INVENTORY · UPDATED REAL-TIME
3
Budget signal: average + median price of past purchases
EX: GOLDBERG AVG $104K · MEDIAN $58K
