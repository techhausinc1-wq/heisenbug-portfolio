→ Shared engine · same code, different shape

Each cockpit borrows from every other cockpit.

The thesis: features built for one operator become available to all. The Furniture cockpit gets Tax & Documents from the Audit Practice. The Diamond Atelier gets the Photo Pipeline from the Auto Parts AutoLister. Everyone gets multilingual chat. The engine is shared; the configuration is bespoke.

Tax & Compliance
Originated in Audit Practice
Now in: Furniture · Diamond Atelier · HVAC · Pool · Auto Parts
Sales tax filing, multi-state nexus tracking, 1099 vendor automation, use tax, luxury tax thresholds (jewelry), service-business tax (pool/HVAC). Same engine, industry-tuned rates and rules.
Memo Cadence Engine
Originated in Diamond Atelier
Now in: Furniture (designer/stager memos) · Pool (equipment loans) · Auto Parts (parts on memo to rebuilders)
7/14/21/30-day automatic follow-up cadence. Day 30 risk flag protects exposure. Adapted per industry: jewelry memos to clients, furniture pieces to stagers, transmissions on memo to body shops.
Photo Pipeline + AI ID
Originated in Auto Parts AutoLister
Now in: Diamond Atelier (stone photography) · HVAC (equipment photos) · Pool (equipment cataloging) · Furniture (new SKU staging)
Shoot → AI identifies → auto-tags → auto-lists. Built for 1,000 photos/day at the source. Stone-cert lookup in jewelry. HVAC nameplate extraction. Same engine; different recognition models per vertical.
Document Inbox + OCR
Originated in Audit Practice
Now in: Furniture (designer contracts, fabric COAs) · Diamond Atelier (certs, provenance) · HVAC (warranty docs) · Pool (HOA contracts) · Auto Parts (titles)
Drag and drop, auto-OCR, auto-routing to the right engagement/order/SKU. Each industry has its own document taxonomy; the engine handles all of them.
Milestone / Anniversary Engine
Originated in Diamond Atelier
Now in: Furniture (move-in / renovation / family events) · HVAC (system replacement cycles) · Auto Parts (vehicle anniversaries)
Proactive outreach based on dates that matter. Jewelry: anniversaries. Furniture: move-in milestones. HVAC: 10-year system replacement reminder. Same engine; different event sources.
Multilingual AI Chat
Originated in Furniture Cockpit
Now in: Diamond Atelier (Russian / Hebrew) · Pool (Spanish) · HVAC (Spanish) · Audit (client comms)
4-language chat handles after-hours inquiries, transfers warm leads to the human. Industry vocabulary tuned per cockpit. Furniture: Polish + Russian + Spanish. Jewelry: Russian + Hebrew. Pool/HVAC: Spanish.
Financing Waterfall
Originated in Furniture Cockpit
Now in: HVAC (equipment financing) · Pool (new build / large equipment) · Auto Parts (engine/transmission payment plans)
5-lender waterfall: Snap → Acima → Affirm → Synchrony → Wells. 60-second decision. Each industry has its own lender mix tuned for the typical transaction size.
Route Optimization
Originated in Pool Service + Vending Route Scout
Now in: Furniture (delivery) · HVAC (service calls) · Auto Parts (local delivery) · Insulation (job site visits)
Daily route optimization with live status updates, photo proof of completion, customer-side ETA tracking. Same engine; vehicle-class / stop-type / time-window rules differ per industry.
Multi-Channel Listing
Originated in Auto Parts AutoLister
Now in: Furniture (Wayfair / Amazon / own store) · Diamond Atelier (1stDibs / Chairish / Etsy)
One source of truth → live everywhere. Auto-pause when sold on any channel. Auto-pricing rules per channel. Channel-margin analysis to see where to push more inventory.
Intent Scout Signals
Originated in Pool Scout + BrewRoute
Now in: Furniture (move-in / renovation) · HVAC (system age data) · Insulation (new construction permits) · Audit (business events triggering planning)
Surface buying signals before competitors see them. New permits, home sales, business events, life moments. The same scout engine that powers location intent for vending also powers anniversary intent for jewelry.

A pool service operator and a diamond house need different software. We build the right tool for the right industry — not a generic CRM with industry branding tacked on.

03
